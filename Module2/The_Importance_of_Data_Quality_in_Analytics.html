How to Spot Good vs. Bad Data

Just like checking fruits for bruises or expiration dates on milk, data has its checks too. Here are some key metrics:

  • Accuracy: Is the data correct?
  • Completeness: Are all pieces of the data there?
  • Consistency: Is the data the same across all sources?
  • Timeliness: Is the data up-to-date?
